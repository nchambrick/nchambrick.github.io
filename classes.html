Types of yoga we offer:

  • Hot 26 - A series of 26 yoga poses and two breathing exercises practiced in a heated room.
  • Yin - A meditative yoga practice that involves holding poses for extended periods of time to stretch and relax the body's connective tissues. It's a slow-paced style of yoga that's based on the Taoist concept of yin and yang.

What is Yoga?

Yoga is an ancient practice that combines physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation to improve health and well-being. The word "yoga" comes from the Sanskrit word yuj, which means "to join" or "to unite". Yoga is a spiritual practice that originated in India. It's a science and art of healthy living that aims to bring harmony between the mind and body. Yoga can improve physical health, such as flexibility, strength, balance, and posture. It can also improve mental health, such as concentration, memory, and mindfulness. Yoga can help develop self-discipline and awareness. There are many different types of yoga, ranging from gentle to more physically demanding.

Elements of yoga
Physical postures:Also known as asanas, these are the physical positions used in yoga. Breathing techniques: Also known as pranayama, these are the breathing exercises used in yoga.
Meditation:Also known as dyana, this is a practice of focusing on the present moment without judgment.

Benefits of yoga
Improved flexibility, strength, balance, and posture Improved concentration and memory Improved health of internal organs Increased mindfulness Reduced stress
